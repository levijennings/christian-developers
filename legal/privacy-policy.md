# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

DVLMNT ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Christian Developers website, job board, community forum, mentorship platform, and related services (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Christian Developers, you consent to our data practices described in this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

**Account Information:** Name, email address, password, location, company affiliation, professional credentials, and optional profile information.

**Profile Information:** Headline, bio, skills, experience, portfolio links, social media profiles, and career interests.

**Job-Related Information:** Resume, cover letter, qualifications, salary expectations, and application history.

**Mentorship Information:** Mentorship preferences, areas of expertise, availability, goals, and relationship history.

**Communications:** Forum posts, prayer requests, mentorship notes, messages, and emails.

**Payment Information:** Billing address, payment method information (processed through Stripe).

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data:** Pages visited, posts read, job applications submitted, mentorship interactions, time spent on Service, clicks, and actions taken.

**Device Information:** Device type, operating system, browser, IP address, and device identifier.

**Cookies and Tracking Technologies:** We use cookies to maintain sessions, remember preferences, and analyze usage.

### 1.3 Third-Party Data Sources

We may receive information from:
- Payment processors (Stripe)
- Analytics providers
- Social media platforms (if you use social login)
- Employment verification services

2.3 **Community Functions:** We use information to:

## 3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 **Public Profile Information:** Your name, headline, location, professional credentials, and optional bio are visible on your public profile. Job seekers may have additional information visible to employers.

3.2 **Job Applications:** When you apply for a job, the employer receives:
- Your resume or application
- Your contact information
- Any information you include in your application

3.3 **Mentorship Connections:** When matched with a mentor or mentee:
- Both parties can see profile information
- Direct messaging enables private communication
- Mentors may have access to mentee career information

3.4 **Forum Visibility:** Forum posts and prayer requests are visible to other community members based on access levels (public or community-only).

3.5 **Service Providers:** We share information with service providers:
- Supabase (database hosting and authentication)
- Stripe (payment processing)
- Email delivery services
- Analytics providers
- Cloud storage providers

Service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.

3.6 **Third-Party Services:**
- **Stripe:** Payment and subscription processing
- **Supabase:** Database and authentication
- **Sentry:** Error tracking and monitoring
- **PostHog:** Analytics and product insights

3.7 **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or legal process, including to:
- Law enforcement and government agencies
- Courts and judicial bodies
- Parties in legal proceedings

3.8 **Business Transfers:** If DVLMNT is acquired or merges, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

3.9 **No Sale:** We do not sell your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 **Storage Location:** Your data is stored on servers managed by Supabase, which may be located in various jurisdictions, including the United States.

4.2 **Security Measures:** We implement industry-standard security:
- Encryption in transit (SSL/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments
- Secure password hashing

However, no security is 100% effective.

4.3 **Data Retention:** We retain information as follows:
- Active accounts: Data retained while account is active
- Terminated accounts: Profile and content removed; some data may be retained for legal compliance
- Payment information: Retained per Stripe's requirements
- Usage data: Typically retained for 12 months
- Forum and community content: Retained unless deleted by user

## 5. Your Privacy Rights

### 5.1 Access and Portability
You have the right to access your personal information and receive it in a portable format. To request this, email levi@dvlmnt.com with "Data Access Request" in the subject line.

### 5.2 Correction and Deletion
You have the right to correct inaccurate information and request deletion of your account and data. We will comply with deletion requests within 30 days (subject to legal retention requirements).

### 5.3 Opt-Out
You may opt out of:
- Marketing emails: Click unsubscribe or manage preferences
- Community notifications: Adjust settings in account preferences
- Data collection: Disable analytics in browser settings

### 5.4 CCPA Rights (California Residents)
If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Delete personal information (subject to exceptions)
- Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell)
- Non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise CCPA rights, contact levi@dvlmnt.com with "CCPA Request" in the subject line.

### 5.5 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)
If you are an EU resident, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:
- Access to your personal data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Restrict processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent
- Lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority

To exercise GDPR rights, contact levi@dvlmnt.com.
