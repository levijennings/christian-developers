# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Christian Developers (operated by DVLMNT) uses cookies and similar tracking technologies, and how you can manage your preferences.

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain information about your browsing activity. They allow websites to remember information about you and improve your experience.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use

### 2.1 Essential Cookies
These are required for Christian Developers to function properly and include:
- Session management and user authentication
- Security tokens to prevent unauthorized access
- Account information and user preferences
- Protection against fraud and abuse
- Language and interface preferences
- Remember login status

**These cannot be disabled without affecting core functionality.**

### 2.2 Analytics Cookies
These help us understand how users interact with Christian Developers:
- **Sentry:** Error tracking and crash reporting
- **PostHog:** Usage analytics and feature adoption
- **Custom Analytics:** Tracking of job board usage, forum activity, and mentorship interactions

These cookies help us identify technical issues and improve the Service.

### 2.3 Functional Cookies
These cookies improve your experience by remembering:
- Your search filters and preferences
- Job postings you've saved
- Forum categories you follow
- Notification preferences
- Mentor/mentee interests and availability
- Dashboard settings

### 2.4 Performance Cookies
These measure how well Christian Developers performs:
- Page load times
- Server response times
- UI responsiveness
- Feature performance metrics

## 4. How to Manage Your Cookies

### 4.1 Browser Settings
Most browsers allow you to control cookies:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy, search, and services > Clear browsing data

You can choose to:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Delete existing cookies
- Set specific preferences by website

### 4.2 Cookie Consent Banner
When you first visit Christian Developers, we display a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- **Accept All:** Accept all cookies
- **Essential Only:** Accept only essential cookies
- **Customize:** Choose specific cookie categories

Your preference is saved in a consent cookie.

### 4.3 Third-Party Opt-Out
You can opt out of some third-party analytics services:
- **PostHog:** https://posthog.com/docs/faq#how-to-opt-out-of-posthog
- **Sentry:** Through browser settings

### 4.4 Do Not Track
If your browser includes a "Do Not Track" (DNT) setting and you enable it, we will limit analytics cookie collection.

## 5. What Happens if You Disable Cookies?

If you disable essential cookies, Christian Developers may not function properly:
- You may not be able to log in or maintain your session
- Your preferences and saved items may not be retained
- Messages and notifications may not function correctly
- Job application progress may not be saved

Disabling analytics cookies will not significantly affect functionality but may prevent us from identifying technical issues.

## 6. Cookie Retention

- **Session cookies:** Deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent cookies:** Typically retained for 1 year unless cleared earlier
- **Consent cookie:** Retained for 1 year (refreshed if you adjust preferences)
- **Analytics cookies:** Retained per analytics provider policies

## 7. Security and Privacy

7.1 **Secure Transmission:** Cookies are transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).

7.2 **No Sensitive Data:** Cookies do not contain passwords, payment information, or other highly sensitive data.

7.3 **Further Information:** See our Privacy Policy for more details about how we collect and use data.

## 8. International Data Transfers

Cookies are stored on your device but data collected through cookies may be transferred internationally. See our Privacy Policy for details on international data transfers.
