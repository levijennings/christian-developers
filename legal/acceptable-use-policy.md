# Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the prohibited activities and conduct standards for users of Christian Developers (operated by DVLMNT). By using Christian Developers, you agree to comply with this policy.

## 1. Prohibited Activities

You agree not to use Christian Developers in any manner that:

### 1.1 Illegal Activities
- Violates any applicable federal, state, local, or international law or regulation
- Involves fraud, embezzlement, theft, or financial crimes
- Constitutes harassment, stalking, discrimination, or abuse
- Involves threats or violence
- Infringes intellectual property rights
- Violates employment or anti-discrimination laws
- Violates privacy or data protection laws

### 1.2 System Interference
- Attempts unauthorized access to Christian Developers or its systems
- Disrupts the normal operation of the Service
- Overloads servers with excessive requests (DDoS attacks)
- Exploits vulnerabilities or bugs
- Interferes with network connectivity
- Transmits malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Uses automated tools to scrape or harvest user data without authorization
- Reverse-engineers the Service or attempts to extract source code

### 1.3 Fraudulent or Deceptive Conduct
- Misrepresents your identity, credentials, or professional background
- Provides false or misleading information on your profile or applications
- Creates fake accounts or impersonates others
- Engages in phishing or social engineering
- Posts false or misleading job postings
- Falsifies payment information
- Manipulates job ratings or reviews artificially

### 1.4 Spam and Abuse
- Sends unsolicited commercial messages or spam
- Posts repetitive, irrelevant, or off-topic content in forums
- Exploits the messaging or notification system
- Creates content designed to manipulate search or recommendation algorithms
- Engages in coordinated inauthentic behavior to artificially inflate engagement

### 1.5 Harassment and Discrimination
- Harasses, bullies, or threatens other users
- Posts content containing hate speech, discrimination, or racism
- Targets individuals or groups based on protected characteristics
- Engages in sexual harassment or unwanted advances
- Creates a hostile environment in community spaces
- Uses the platform to coordinate harassment or abuse
- Doxes or publicly shares others' private information

### 1.6 Intellectual Property Violations
- Uploads or shares copyrighted content without authorization
- Violates trademark rights or brand guidelines
- Shares trade secrets or confidential business information
- Uses others' content without attribution or permission
- Plagiarizes or misrepresents authorship

## 2. Product-Specific Rules

### 2.1 Job Board

**Employers agree to:**
- Post legitimate job opportunities from your organization
- Provide accurate and complete job descriptions
- Not engage in discriminatory hiring practices
- Comply with all employment laws and regulations
- Use applicant information only for hiring purposes
- Respond professionally to job applicants
- Not solicit personal financial information from applicants

**Employers agree NOT to:**
- Post fake or non-existent job opportunities
- Use job postings for marketing or data collection unrelated to hiring
- Discriminate based on protected characteristics
- Engage in predatory hiring or pyramid schemes
- Share applicant information with unauthorized third parties
- Violate applicant privacy or confidentiality
- Conduct background checks without proper consent and disclosure

**Job Applicants agree to:**
- Provide truthful information in applications and resumes
- Not apply multiple times for the same position from different accounts
- Represent your qualifications and experience accurately
- Respect employer confidentiality and application privacy
- Comply with background check and verification processes
- Not disclose confidential company information from previous employers

**Job Applicants agree NOT to:**
- Provide false or misleading information in applications
- Misrepresent qualifications or experience
- Submit spam applications or untargeted bulk applications
- Share job postings or employer information inappropriately
- Disclose confidential information from previous employers
- Apply using fake identities or impersonation

### 2.2 Community Forum

**All Members agree to:**
- Respect the faith-based nature of the community
- Treat all members with dignity and respect
- Engage in constructive dialogue and healthy debate
- Comply with community guidelines and content standards
- Assume good faith in other members' communications
- Respect diverse perspectives and Christian traditions
- Report violations to moderators

**All Members agree NOT to:**
- Post hateful, discriminatory, or dehumanizing content
- Harass or bully other members
- Disrupt discussions with spam or off-topic content
- Share personal information about others without consent
- Post sexually explicit or pornographic content
- Promote illegal activities or violence
- Impersonate other members or moderators
- Spread misinformation or conspiracy theories
- Doxx or publicly share others' information
- Engage in political activism unrelated to faith and work

### 2.3 Prayer Requests

**Members who post prayer requests agree to:**
- Share genuine personal prayer needs
- Maintain confidentiality of prayer requests if marked private
- Provide only information you're comfortable sharing publicly
- Understand that prayer requests may be shared within the community

**Members responding to prayer requests agree to:**
- Respond with compassion and support
- Not use prayer requests for proselytizing or agenda-pushing
- Respect the confidentiality of shared information
- Maintain appropriate boundaries
- Not provide medical, legal, or professional advice
- Avoid judgment or criticism

**Both parties agree NOT to:**
- Mock or ridicule prayer requests or requesters
- Use prayer requests for spam or marketing
- Exploit shared information for personal gain
- Violate the confidence and trust of the community
- Share requests outside the community without permission
- Encourage harmful or dangerous actions

### 2.4 Mentorship Relationships

**Mentors agree to:**
- Provide professional, honest guidance and support
- Commit to agreed-upon meeting frequency and duration
- Maintain appropriate professional boundaries
- Respect mentee confidentiality and privacy
- Disclose conflicts of interest
- Provide constructive feedback
- Be accessible and responsive

**Mentors agree NOT to:**
- Exploit the mentorship relationship for personal gain
- Engage in romantic or sexual advances
- Abuse the mentor position to pressure mentees
- Share mentee information without consent
- Fail to maintain appropriate boundaries
- Violate mentee confidentiality
- Engage in quid pro quo arrangements

**Mentees agree to:**
- Respect the mentor's time and commitments
- Prepare for mentorship meetings
- Act on advice and feedback provided
- Maintain confidentiality of discussions
- Communicate openly about expectations
- Terminate the relationship respectfully if needed

**Mentees agree NOT to:**
- Violate the mentor's privacy or confidentiality
- Exploit the mentorship relationship
- Engage in romantic or sexual advances
- Share mentorship discussions without consent
- Fail to respect boundaries
- Misrepresent mentorship to others

## 3. Content Standards

All user-generated content must:

3.1 **Be Honest:** Content must not contain false, misleading, or deceptive information.

3.2 **Be Respectful:** Content must not demean, harass, or attack individuals or groups.

3.3 **Be Appropriate:** Content must comply with laws and community standards.

3.4 **Protect Privacy:** Content must not disclose private information about individuals without consent.

3.5 **Respect IP:** Content must not infringe intellectual property rights of others.

3.6 **Respect Faith:** Content must be respectful of Christian faith and diverse denominational perspectives within Christianity.

## 4. Community Standards - Faith and Inclusion

4.1 **Faith-Inclusive Environment:** Christian Developers welcomes Christians of all denominational backgrounds and traditions. Users agree to:
- Respect diverse Christian perspectives and practices
- Avoid denominational dismissiveness or contempt
- Recognize that theological differences are normal and acceptable
- Engage thoughtfully with different Christian traditions
- Maintain a welcoming environment for all believers

4.2 **What This Means:**
- Doctrine and theology discussions are welcome if conducted respectfully
- Critiquing other denominations or traditions respectfully is acceptable
- Dismissing entire groups of Christians as non-believers is not acceptable
- Requiring agreement on non-core doctrines as a condition of community membership is not acceptable
- Assuming that all Christian developers share your specific theological views is not acceptable

4.3 **Prohibited Religious Conduct:**
- Declaring other Christian denominations as non-Christian or heretical in dismissive ways
- Using the platform to recruit for your specific church or denomination
- Creating division based on theological minutiae
- Requiring religious loyalty tests as a condition of community participation
- Harassing members based on their specific Christian tradition
- Suggesting members will face judgment or damnation based on their theological views

## 5. Enforcement and Consequences

### 5.1 Monitoring
DVLMNT monitors user activity and content for compliance with this AUP, including automated and manual review.

### 5.2 Content Removal
We may remove content that violates this AUP, including:
- False or misleading job postings
- Harassing or discriminatory forum posts
- Spam or spam-like content
- Sexually explicit content
- Content promoting illegal activities
- Privacy violations
- Intellectual property infringement

Content removal occurs without prior notice when violations are clear.

### 5.3 Account Warnings
For minor violations, we may issue a warning describing the violation and requesting corrective action.

### 5.4 Account Suspension
For serious or repeated violations, we may temporarily suspend your account, restricting:
- Posting in forums or prayer requests
- Submitting job postings or applications
- Accessing mentorship features
- Private messaging with other members

### 5.5 Account Termination
We may permanently terminate your account if you:
- Repeatedly violate this AUP despite warnings
- Commit serious violations (harassment, discrimination, fraud)
- Attempt to circumvent suspension restrictions
- Engage in illegal activity
- Pose a threat to other users or the Service

### 5.6 Legal Action
For serious violations including illegal activity or harassment, we may pursue legal action and cooperate with law enforcement.

## 6. Reporting Violations

6.1 To report violations, please:
- Use in-app reporting features on specific content
- Email levi@dvlmnt.com with "AUP Violation Report" in the subject line

6.2 Your report should include:
- Description of the violation
- Reference or link to the violating content
- Your contact information
- Supporting evidence if available

6.3 We will investigate promptly and take appropriate action.

6.4 False or bad-faith reports may result in warnings or account suspension.

## 7. Appeals

7.1 If your account has been suspended or terminated, you may appeal within 30 days by emailing levi@dvlmnt.com with "Account Appeal" in the subject line.

7.2 Your appeal should explain why you believe the action was inappropriate and demonstrate commitment to future compliance.

7.3 We will review appeals within 14 days and notify you of the outcome.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

DVLMNT reserves the right to modify this AUP at any time. Changes are effective upon posting. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of modifications.

9.2 **Severability:** If any provision is found unenforceable, remaining provisions continue in effect.

9.3 **No Waiver:** Failure to enforce this AUP does not constitute a waiver of DVLMNT's rights.
